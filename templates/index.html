⚠️ Dangers of AI in Classrooms

Understand the risks before integrating AI into education

⚠️ Data Privacy

AI tools may collect and store sensitive student data. Misuse or breach can expose personal information.

🤖 Bias in AI Models

AI tools may reinforce racial, gender, or socio-economic bias if trained on non-inclusive datasets.

📉 Over-reliance on AI

Students may become dependent on AI tools for tasks, reducing their critical thinking and learning depth.

🧠 Dehumanized Learning

Replacing teachers or mentors with AI can strip away the emotional intelligence and adaptability of human interaction.

🔍 Lack of Transparency

Many AI systems operate as black boxes. Educators and students may not understand how decisions are made.
